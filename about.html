About e_Touring

Connecting people with nature through sustainable eco-tourism

Who We Are

e_Touring is an eco-tourism initiative based in Lebanon, dedicated to creating meaningful nature experiences such as camping, hiking, and adventure tours.

We aim to connect people with nature while promoting environmental awareness, sustainability, and community involvement.

Our activities are designed not only to explore breathtaking landscapes but also to support local traditions and empower rural communities.

Our Mission

To design and organize eco-tourism experiences that blend fun, discovery and environmental education.

To empower local communities by involving them in eco-tourism activities and promoting sustainability.

To encourage every traveler to become a nature ambassador through responsible and consious tourism.

Our Vision

To become a leading platform that inspires sustainable exploration of nature through meaningful eco-friendly adventures.
