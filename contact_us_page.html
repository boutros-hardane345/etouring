Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book an event?

You can book events directly through our website by clicking "Book via WhatsApp" on any event. You'll be connected to our team to complete your booking.

What should I bring to a hike?

We recommend bringing: comfortable hiking shoes, weather-appropriate clothing, water bottle, snacks, sun protection, and a small backpack. We'll send you a detailed list after booking.

Are your events suitable for beginners?

Yes! We offer events for all skill levels. Each event listing includes a difficulty rating (Easy, Medium, Hard) to help you choose the right experience.
